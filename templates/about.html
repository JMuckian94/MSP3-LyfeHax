About Us

This site is the place where users can post and learn amazing tips and tricks to get them through life. All of these hacks are DIY and should at a touch of flair to your problem solving. Aiming to put a communities collective heads together to fix problems is our primary goal with this site.

What is Considered a Lifehack?

A lifehack by definition is "a strategy or technique adopted in order to manage one's time and daily activities in a more efficient way." They are tips and tricks, either old tried and tested strategies or new inventive methods, intended to free up your valuable time for other more important things in life!
