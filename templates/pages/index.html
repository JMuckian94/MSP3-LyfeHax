What is this site all about?


This site is the place where users can post and learn amazing tips and tricks to get them through life. All of these lifehacks or "hax" are DIY and should add new tools to your problem solving toolkit. We aim to put a communities collective heads together to fix problems and free up time for the important things in life!

What is a Lifehack?


A lifehack by definition is "a strategy or technique adopted in order to manage one's time and daily activities in a more efficient way." They are tips and tricks, either old tried and tested strategies or new inventive methods, intended to free up your valuable time for other more important things in life!
